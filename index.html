Our Story

Born from a vegetarian athlete's need for transparent nutrition, Micro Pantry began when Vedant discovered his protein shakes contained harmful artificial sweeteners that traditional nutrition apps ignored.

What started as a personal solution has evolved into a revolutionary platform that goes beyond calorie counting to provide comprehensive nutritional insights. We're not just tracking food—we're transforming how people understand and interact with their nutrition.

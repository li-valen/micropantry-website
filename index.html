Why Us

At Micro Pantry, we go beyond traditional nutrition tracking.

While most apps simply count calories or macronutrients, we provide a holistic approach to nutrition by offering detailed insights into the ingredients you’re consuming—revealing the hidden effects of additives, sweeteners, and other micronutrients that traditional trackers overlook.

Additionally, our platform is designed to reduce food waste by helping you make the most of what’s already in your pantry, ensuring that food is used efficiently and responsibly.

Virtual Pantry and Meal Tracking

Track the items in your pantry and meals stored in our database with the help of AI image detection. Simply take pictures of your meals, pantry, or barcodes, and the system will automatically track these items for you.

Micro-nutritional Tracking

By combining our unique web-scraping algorithm with extensive food APIs, we can accurately track the micronutritional content of your meals and ingredients. Building on the previously mentioned image detection technology, this process is easy, efficient, and designed to streamline healthy eating.

Personalized Meal Recommendations

Building on the previous feature, we utilize generative AI to provide personalized advice on recipes and meals that best support healthy eating. These recommendations are tailored specifically to your goals, body type, allergies, and other dietary needs, with adaptive learning to continuously improve and refine the suggestions.

Our Story

Micro Pantry was born out of a personal need for transparency in nutrition and better food management. As a vegetarian athlete, Vedant relied on a popular nutrition app to track his diet, only to realize that it focused solely on calories and macronutrients, while ignoring the hidden risks of processed ingredients. Vedant discovered that his daily supplemental protein shakes contain artificial sweeteners linked to health risks.

Frustrated by the gaps in existing solutions, Vedant set out to create something better. Beginning as just a concept in the Winter of last year, Micro Pantry grew into a much larger effort to redefine dietary culture.

We wanted to move beyond the idea that food is just a set of numbers or a way to hit a target. Instead, we aimed to help people become more aware of how their food choices impact their health. What started as a solution for our own needs has evolved into a game-changing tool for athletes, health-conscious individuals, and anyone looking to take control of their food choices. At Micro Pantry, we don’t just track food—we redefine how you interact with it.
